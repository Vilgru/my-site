Team Fortress 3 is the future Valve game releasing in 2080.

Just like TF2, It will be always a First-Person shooter multiplayer game. This game will consist of two teams: RED and BLU.

There will be the same types of gamemodes:

-Payload

-King Of The Hill

Attack/Defense

-Capture The Flag

-Payload Race

And the alternative gamemodes.

There are nine classes will still be included: Scout, Soldier, Pyro, Demoman, Heavy, Engineer, Medic, Sniper and Spy.

Features for TF3:

-The game will have a full VAC system and instantly detects bots and prevent cheaters from joining any servers.

-Enable all chat features for “Free to play” players.
